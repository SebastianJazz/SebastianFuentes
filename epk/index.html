Biography

Chilean jazz guitarist, composer, and educator based in Boston, USA. His main influences are Tim Miller and Adam Rogers, and his compositions are influenced by jazz orchestra writers such Maria Schneider, and Bob Brookmeyer. In 2012, he received his first bachelor’s degree in Composition at Escuela Moderna de Música in Chile, where he also served as a professor. Sebastian has also taught at nonprofit organizations. Sebastian has performed in various jazz festivals such as the Panama Jazz Festival, Chile Jazz por la Paz, Chileuropa, among others. In 2020, Sebastian received his second bachelor’s degree from Berklee College of Music, where he studied with Tim Miller and Mick Goodrick. Sebastian studied at Berklee Global Jazz Institute (BGJI) under the mentorship of Danilo Perez, Joe Lovano, John Patitucci, Terri Lyne Carrington, Dave Liebman, George Garzone, Adam Cruz, Ben Street, Marco Pignataro, among others. Sebastian is currently studying a master’s program at BGJI.
